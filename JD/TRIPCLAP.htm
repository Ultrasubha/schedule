About Company

We are a bunch of travelpreneurs with more than 50yrs of combined experience in travel industry. We aim to become India's number one travel marketplace where travelers can book any travel service directly via local service provider and get instant confirmation. This reduces middle layer and ensures better quality of service. We want to Empower more than 1 million travel sme with right tools and platform so that they can provide best in class service to every class of traveler. Our efforts reduces middle layer which directly benefits both travelers and service providers.
