About Company

An international higher education platform for students where we own the entire experience of college life - right from admission counseling, academic guidance to online certifications/courses and work-study programs along with the college degree courses. We are driven with a vision of higher education reform by making quality education in India affordable and giving students skill-based training which leads to jobs. We have developed a hybrid, asset-light university model offering accredited, employable, affordable college education for the middle 60% of the student population and are currently seeing phenomenal growth in African and saarc countries. We are a Delhi-based growth-stage startup in the education technology domain looking for like-minded people, enthusiastic about working in education domain and/or industry-defining new businesses/startups. We have recently found great traction with our product EDOOFA and our exclusive partnership with the ministry of higher education in Bhutan has given us a monopoly in an emerging market. We are now seeing phenomenal growth from India, Africa (Ghana, Nigeria, Kenya, Zimbabwe, Rwanda, Uganda), and Saarc (Afghanistan, Bhutan, Nepal, Bangladesh). - African Corporate Alliance (B2B), Academic Happiness Officer/ International Student Mentor, Business Operations Analyst/ Lead- Operations Analyst, Associate- Key Account Management, Social Media and PR/Content Manager

Job Profile

Designation : Business Development - African Corporate Alliance (B2B), Academic Happiness Officer/ International Student Mentor, Business Operations Analyst/ Lead- Operations Analyst, Associate- Key Account Management, Social Media and PR/Content Manager

1) Business Development - African Corporate Alliance (B2B):
Responsibilities:
** Prospection of a potential client via detailed analysis
** Research and Identify new partnership opportunities
** Take complete ownership of the process- from the first conversation with a potential client to the closure
** Prepare and deliver pitches to potential clients
** Create mutually beneficial proposals for long-term associations with clients
** Negotiate with clients for closures and partnership opportunities
** Maintain fruitful relationships with clients and address their needs for retention
** Meet and surpass business expansion/revenue expectations for the overall sustainable growth of the business.

2) Academic Happiness Officer/ International Student Mentor:
** Coordinate with the enrollment/KAM team to understand a particular account's (student) details before mentoring them
** Creating project outlines along with the project management/SEELS team for efficient project delivery through students
** Training and mentoring them at every step to create an impact on their skill set.
** Helping students earn scholarship credits by helping them deliver projects
** Create and curate curriculum and content for training on a regular basis
** Coordinate with senior mentors to improve a student's overall growth as a professional


3) Business Operations Analyst/ Lead- Operations Analyst:
** Strong analytical mindset to understanding trends and patterns through data
** Initiative-taking capabilities to find and solve problems at scale
** Organized approach with attention to detail
** Consultative and team player approach
** Out-of-the-box thinking
** Strong knowledge and experience with tools (SQL, google analytics, Excel, Tableau), databases, and programming (Knowledge of MERN framework is an addedplus - Mongo DB, Express, React JS, Node JS)
** Experience in product analytics and innovation capabilities
** Passionate about individual growth in a flexible environment

4)  Associate- Key Account Management:
** Identification of suitable leads for KAM with respect to students' background verification and analysis.
** To design the communication process for orientation of academic norms including formal requirements.
** To mentor students and their guardians about the admission and enrollment procedures in accordance with their academic interests and challenges.
** To carry on regular follow-ups and solve process-related queries/concerns with urgency with thorough documentation and reporting structure.
** To help the students by designing the financial plan for further admission evaluation and procedures.
** To help the students interact with the Universities and optimize admission formalities as per the deadlines.
** To collaborate with internal cross-functional teams (Sales and EWYL) for critical evaluation and scrutiny of the student's progress and status.
** To ensure the positive conversions of enrolled students into registrations by attaining client-focused solutions.

5) Social Media and PR/Content Manager
** Develop social media content plans for IG and FB and LinkedIn that are consistent with the company's brand identity.
** Creating consistent, meaningful content on the platform, including writing and editing social media posts, improving customer engagement, and promoting social media campaigns.
** Hands-on usage of editing tools like Canva, Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, etc.
** Communicating with social media followers, including responding to queries in a timely manner.
** Developing and managing social influencer programs and attending social influencer events.
** Using analytical tools to monitor and evaluate the company's social media presence and performance.
** Preparing monthly & weekly reports on social media marketing efforts.
** Suggest recommendations to adjust the social media marketing strategy for optimal results.
** Staying up to date on best practices and emerging trends in social media.
** Building and promoting brand identity by identifying and participating in networking events
** Creating common and scalable strategies for managing team communication on socials

Skills :
** Organized, analytical skills with attention to detail
** Exceptional written and spoken communication
** Exceptional persuasion and negotiation skills along with a student-first approach
** Passionate about individual growth in a flexible environment
** Leadership, Independent functioning, and Initiative taking abilities
** Problem-Solving and Consultative approach
** Out-of-the-box thinking, creative content writing skills
** Creative Educator approach toward students
** Data-driven approach - recording and maintaining data regularly
