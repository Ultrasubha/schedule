About Company

We are an Ed-Tech startup growing rapidly since its inception. We are redefining e-learning and have created an AI-based model that caters to the K-12 to K-16 space. Externsclub has had the distinct advantage of working with the student community and understanding their real needs. Our products and services are designed around catering to the very specific knowledge gaps the young student faces. A knowledge platform that can be accessed from anywhere in the world, so that no student is left behind, this is the model that helps us realize its mission of democratizing education.
