About Company

GoComet is the world’s most easy-to-use, intuitive multi-modal logistics platform that enables global businesses to automate their end-end logistics profitably. The AI-powered, cloud-based multi-modal logistics platform is being used by large manufacturing conglomerates to automate various processes across the supply chain, such as procuring freight rates, tracking shipments, reconciling invoices, benchmarking freight, and more. GoComet is helping global companies move cargo worth billions of dollars every year and has already enabled its worldwide customers to achieve double-digit cost savings through its platform. Today, GoComet is trusted by 150+ global brands across 35 countries. Our customers include leading international companies such as Sun Pharma, Sapmer, Glenmark, Polyplex, Alliance Tyres (Yokohama Tyres’ subsidiary), Lupin, ACG, to name a few. GoComet is currently headquartered in Singapore and has a presence in Southeast Asia, the US and Europe. GoComet has received many accolades from renowned industry associations in the last two years. The platform has been recognised as the top transport management solution and mentioned in leading publications such as Forbes, Gartner, The Straits Times, Business Today, Logistics Tech Outlook USA, and more.
