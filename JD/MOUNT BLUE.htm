About Company

MountBlue is a launching pad for people looking to add a major boost to  their software engineering career at starting itself. MountBlue helps companies (primarily product startups) with their entry-level tech hiring needs. They run coding boot camps for entry-level developers on the most popular web and mobile technologies, and then find them rewarding software careers in startups. Their developers are now employees in some of the most well-known startups in India - from bootstrapped upstarts to unicorns. Some of their customers are Razorpay, Zomato, Rivigo, Freshworks, OkCredit, Acko, Naukri, DailyHunt, Great Learning, Homelane, Instamojo, Magicpin etc. They work with ~180 startups and this is an ever-growing number
