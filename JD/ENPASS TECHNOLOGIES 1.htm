About Company

Enpass Technologies Inc is a US based, product focused company working in the domain of security and authentication. The core product of company is Enpass Password Manager. We are a diverse team of 35 talented people including developers, test engineers, UX designers and product support engineers, and now looking to expand our team in all departments.

Job Role:
** Formulation and implementation of Software Testing strategies.
** Develop and execute a test cases for the tasks, dependencies.
** Involved with test planning, writing the test cases and filing explanatory bug reports.
** Assessing the bug reports by customers on various platforms and plan for the reproduction.
** Working closely to understand the functionality during the planning stage and then devise positive and negative test strategies.
** Being responsible for the different documents linked to the definition, monitoring and control of test and acceptance test activities.
** Understanding the testing effort and planning the resources accordingly.
** Ensuring test quality through optimization of test efficiency and effectiveness.
** Brainstorming, contributing new ideas, thinking of innovative strategies, and implementing solutions to complex problems.
** Working with fellow designers and developers in an iterative environment.
** Working with real-time feedback from our users to make the product better.
** Discover, design, develop, deploy, debug. Repeat!
