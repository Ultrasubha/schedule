Job Profile

React JS Developer (Frontend Developer)
NowPurchase is looking for a highly motivated and enthusiastic React JS Developer to join our front- end development team. In this role, you will be responsible for developing and implementing user interface components using React.js concepts and workflows such as Redux, Flux, and Webpack. You will also be responsible for profiling and improving front-end performance and documenting our front- end codebase and will also have the opportunity to learn and grow your skills in React JS, Redux, and Webpack.

What are the expectations from the role:
 ** Collaborate with the development team to develop and maintain web applications using React JS.
** Write clean, efficient, and maintainable code.
** Implement new features and enhancements to existing applications.
** Participate in code reviews to ensure code quality and maintainability.
** Optimize web applications for maximum speed and scalability.
** Contribute to the documentation of technical solutions and user guides

Qualification and Experience:
 ** Strong understanding of React JS and its core principles.
** Experience with Redux and Webpack is preferred.
** Understanding of front-end technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
** Familiarity with RESTful APIs
** Strong programming background, OOPs, design patterns and data structures
** Should have good analytical and strong problem-solving skills.
** Familiarity with agile development methodologies.
** Experience with test-driven development.
** If you are passionate about web development and eager to learn and grow in a fast-paced startup environment, we encourage you to apply.

Compensation & Benefits:
 ** ESOPs: Your package would entail some ESOPs so that you own a part of the company and grow along with it.
** Medical Insurance: Benefits of group insurance of 3 lakhs for family including parents, spouse, children.
** Generous leave structure and WFHs
** Complete Relocation Expenses: We are building a unicorn out of the city of Joy (Kolkata). We will pay for everything you spend to relocate here.

Possible Growth path: Frontend Engineer (SDE-I) > Senior Frontend Engineer (SDE-II)> Lead Frontend Engineer (SDE-III) > Engineering Manager

Tech Stack:
 ** React/Redux
** Python/Django/Celery
** Git, AWS, ELB (Elastic Beanstalk), Netlify
