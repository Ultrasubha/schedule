About the Role: 
 As a Graphic Design Intern, you will collaborate with our dynamic marketing and design teams to develop, conceptualize, and visualize content for various marketing and content channels/platforms. This role is specifically designed for college students who are passionate about marketing, design, and seeking real-world experience in a creative environment.

Projects You’ll Work on: 
 ** Product Design: Help design & maintain the graphics, mockups, and labels of our white-label products.
** Banner Design: Work with the marketing team on the promotional web, social media, advertising banners and other brand marketing material.
** Illustration Design: Create static & motion illustrations for web UI, app icons, and promotional material from scratch.

Roles and Responsibilities: 
 ** Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including developers, product managers, and marketing, to ensure design alignment and successful project outcomes.
** Assist in the creation and refinement of visual assets, including illustrations, icons, and graphics for our product.
** Develop and strengthen brand assets including style and brand guidelines
** Maintain a set of design best practices and guidelines to reduce and minimize production efforts 
** Produce creative assets including graphics, templates, and layouts and visual standards for marketing activities across multiple channels. These include web, social media, events, email campaigns, and other activations
** Researching on latest design trends to create visuals for our brand
** Solving the marketing and business problems through designing visuals by putting on your creative hats

Required Skills: 
 ** Basic knowledge of graphic design principles and tools.
** Proficiency in graphic design software such as Adobe Creative Suite (Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign) or other similar tools.
** Strong creativity and attention to detail.
** Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment.
