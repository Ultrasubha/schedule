About Company

TridentGroup is a diversified group of businesses headquartered in Ludhiana of Punjab State in India. Trident Limited (Trident) is the flagship company of TridentGroup, a USD 1 billion Indian business conglomerate. The group operates in five business segments: Yarn, Bed Linen, Paper, Chemicals and Captive Power.[3] Trident Limited, the flagship company of the group has its customer base in more than 100 countries across 6 continents. Trident is one of the largest yarn spinners in India, one of the world's largest terry towel manufacturers and the world's largest wheat straw based paper manufacturer.
