About Company

ANALITICA is on a mission to help organizations navigate complex data and maximize the  role of data in driving business growth. We partner with companies to connect data & analytics processes to the Final Mile of the decision-making process. As a member of our  Early Founding Team, you will focus on solving organizational problems by analyzing  data in a structured manner. The ideal candidate is comfortable working in an early-stage start-up, working directly with clients across B2B and B2C industries. Here is a ground  floor level opportunity to be part of a growing company that aims to impact decision  making using data in large organizations.  We are looking for a Business Analyst - Intern to support our client success team. The intern will work with a Domain Consultant and own the delivery of solutions. They are critical in framing analysis requirements, assessing data availability and leading solution  development. The intern works along with a team of data analysts in delivering quality  outputs to our clients.

Job ProfileJob Profile:
Objectives of this role:
** We are recruiting Business Analysts to support our client operations.
** The role participates in problem framing, translating business problems into data requirements and executing data analysis working closely with our client servicing team and senior VP level clients.
** Our business analysts are our eyes and ears of the data.

Responsibilities:
** Interpret data, analyse results using analytics, research methodologies, and statistical techniques.
** Develop and implement data analyses, leverage data collection systems and other strategies that optimise statistical efficiency and quality
** Work in tandem with consultants in making meaningful insights from data and analysis for senior clients.
** Prepare, analyse, and summarise various weekly, monthly, and periodic results for use by various key stakeholders.
** Conduct full lifecycle of analytics projects, including extracting, manipulating, and exporting data from project requirements documentation to design and execution
** Evaluate key performance indicators, provide ongoing reports, and recommend business plan updates.

Skills and Qualifications:
** Pursuing bachelor’s degree, preferably in computer science, engineeringmathematics, business management, or economics.
** Basic analytical skills with experience collecting, organising, analysing, and disseminating abundant information with accuracy
** Good verbal, written, and visual communication skills.
** Familiarity with data management tools and scripts – e.g., Excel, SQL, R, PowerBI, Tableau etc.

What you will get:
** A ground-floor level opportunity of working at and building a start-up. You are one of our first team members.
** Exposure to multiple industries through their data and enterprise level business problems.
** Learn the Art of problem solving through data for large enterprises.
** Working for a ‘Remote-first’ organisation and international clientele
** Competitive Compensation.
** Play a role in organisation building.
