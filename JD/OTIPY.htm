About Company

Otipy believes in helping farmers by providing their fresh produce directly to the consumers. We are known as India’s fastest delivery app, which delivers directly from farm to fork in 12 hours only. Along with farm fresh fruits and vegetables we offer variety of groceries, bakery products, dairy goods, home care and many more items.Our team collects the fresh fruits and vegetables directly from the farm. Then at warehouse, products are passed through Quality checking test and the best are selected and packed in eco-friendly packs. We work specifically on technology predictive algorithm to offer the best prices to our consumers and to avoid the wastage.
