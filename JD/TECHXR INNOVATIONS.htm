About Company

We believe that Augmented and Virtual reality technology is an extremely potential tool in the fields of Education, Training, Architecture, Healthcare, Tourism, Remote Collaboration, Fashion, Security, and Gaming. However, due to costly hardware, and lack of affordable courses and content, its adoption is hampered in countries, especially the developing ones like India. But, with our pocket-friendly patented hardware and exclusively designed learning resources, we envision making India a superpower in AR-VR content generation and aim to build the world's largest AR-VR Developer's base in India.

Job ProfileRoles and Responsibilities:
** Design visually appealing UI/UX for immersive AR/VR applications and content.
** Collaborate with Unity developers to integrate and refine UI/UX designs within Unity.
** Polish content and applications on Unity, ensuring a seamless UI/UX experience.
** Conduct user research and testing to optimize UI/UX designs within Unity environments.
** Communicate and collaborate with cross-functional teams, aligning on project requirements and goals.

Requirements:
** A portfolio showcasing UI/UX design work for both web and mobile platforms (2D or 3D)
** Demonstrated ability to create visually appealing and intuitive UI/UX designs
** Expertise in design tools like Figma, XD and Adobe Creative Suite. Unity3D UI/UX is a plus
** Familiarity with Design Systems and 2D-3D experience designing for Android and iOS platforms
** Ability to work both collaboratively as a team player and independently
** Excellent written and verbal communication skills
** Strong multitasking and time-management skills with the ability to prioritize tasks
