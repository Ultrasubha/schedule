Job Description- One Data and Artificial Intelligence
Preferred Course: BE/B Tech/ Non - Circuit Branches
Key Responsibilities:
** Creation of RFQ Packages - Collect relevant information such as Approved Vendor lists, drawings, specifications, and checklists. Prepare comprehensive Request for Quotation (RFQ) packages for manufacturing parts across different commodities.
** Purchase Order Management - Issue Purchase Orders (POs) based on demands or Purchase Requisitions received from the engineering or planning teams.
** Order Fulfillment - Collaborate closely with suppliers, logistics, and fulfillment teams to ensure orders are fulfilled as per the Need Date or Promise Date
** Order Schedule Adjustment - Adjust order schedules based on urgency and communicate changes to relevant stakeholders.
** KPIs and SLAs - Adhere to Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) and business Service Level Agreements (SLAs) related to order fulfillment and procurement processes.
** Supplier Invoice Resolution - Assist suppliers in resolving any outstanding invoices that have not been paid, ensuring proper documentation and communication.
** Cross-Functional Collaboration - Collaborate with various internal teams, including Warehouse, Sourcing, Quality, and Finance, to address and resolve material discrepancies efficiently.
** Material Discrepancy Resolution - Work towards resolving material discrepancies to prevent delays in shipping and ensure smooth supply chain operations.
** KPI Reporting - Prepare and report KPIs and SLAs related to the procurement and supply chain process, providing insights into performance and areas for improvement. Overall, this role involves strong communication, attention to detail, collaboration, and a deep understanding of procurement processes and supply chain dynamics.
