We are hiring interns across all the domains:
1. Industrial Design Intern
Description: Help design and optimize the physical components and ergonomics of our robotic systems.
Skills: Knowledge of CAD software, material science, understanding of manufacturing processes.

2. Graphic Design Intern
Description: Create visual concepts to communicate ideas that inspire, inform, and captivate consumers.
Skills: Proficiency in graphic design software, creativity, understanding of color, text style, and layout.

3. Industrial Robotics Intern
Description: Assist in designing, programming, and implementing automated systems in an industrial setting.
Skills: Familiarity with industrial robotics, understanding of kinematics and dynamics, experience with programming languages (Python/C++).

4. Web Development Intern
Description: Assist in the development and design of our company's web presence.
Skills: Knowledge of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and web development frameworks.

5. Software Front-End Development Intern
Description: Help build intuitive, functional user interfaces for our software products.
Skills: Familiarity with front-end languages (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) and frameworks (React, Vue, Angular).

6. Software Back-End Development Intern
Description: Assist in server-side web application logic and integration of the work front-end developers do.
Skills: Proficiency in server-side languages (e.g., Python, Java, .NET) and databases (SQL, MongoDB).

7. Application Development Intern
Description: Assist in the use case development of new robots and troubleshoot the issues evolving during the development.
Skills: Proficiency in robot programming and system integration, experience with vision systems, End of ARM tools, PLC programming, communication interfacing,

8. Projects Intern
Description: Support in commissioning of robotics projects on time and within budget.
Skills: Understanding of Industrial robotic project execution, organizational skills, proficiency in project management, handling challenges on field at client end.

9. Controls Intern
Description: Assist in the design and programming of system controls for our robotic systems.
Skills: control theory, Understanding of Inverse Kinematics, dynamics, experience with control systems software, familiarity with Python/C++, PLC programming,

10. Embedded Systems Intern
Description: Support the development of hardware-software integration for our robotic systems.
Skills: Understanding of microcontroller programming, knowledge of embedded C/C++, familiarity with hardware-software interfaces.

11. Sales Intern
Description: Assist in identifying business opportunities, building client relationships, and achieving sales goals.
Skills Required: Strong communication skills, understanding of sales principles, ability to work in a team, and a customer-oriented approach.

12. Business Development Intern
Description: Support the growth and expansion of the company by identifying new business opportunities, partnerships, and market trends.
Skills Required: Strong analytical skills, understanding of market research, excellent communication abilities, and a proactive approach to problem-solving.

13. Founder's Office Intern
Description: Work closely with the company's founders to assist in strategic planning, project management, and daily operations.
Skills Required: Excellent organizational skills, ability to multitask, strong communication abilities, discretion, and a keen understanding of business processes.

14. Community Development Intern
Description: Assist in building and nurturing the Orangewood community by engaging with members, organizing events, and creating content.
Skills Required: Strong interpersonal skills, understanding of community engagement strategies, creativity, and experience with social media platforms.
