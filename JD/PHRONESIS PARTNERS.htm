About Company

Phronesis Partners is one of the largest integrated independent research agencies today. Through its global offices in Singapore (Headquarter), Noida, London and Boston, it delivers unique and actionable intelligence to businesses across the world. The company's mission is to "simplify growth" for its partners by giving them data they can rely on intelligence they can act on and decisions they can execute for making a positive difference. Phronesis has been enabling the world's largest financial services, consulting, publication and technology firms delivering over 700 + research projects annually.
