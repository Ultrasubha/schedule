About Company

MyWays, a venture started by IIT Delhi alumni, aims to empower college students and freshers through AI based Adaptive, Practical and Mentored Pre-Career Experiences. We are building a Career Growth Platform to foster Introspection, Information and Interaction to enable holistic and comprehensive career journeys for today’s youth. Our core technology, The EPS - Employability Positioning System, acts as the GPS for Career Navigation. We truly believe that a successful career is a journey and not a destination and hence, believe in helping students become "Experienced Freshers.

Job ProfileResponsibilities:
** Design and Develop ML Models: Utilize your hands-on knowledge of NLP or Computer Vision to design, build, and deploy effective machine learning models.
** Utilize PyTorch: Leverage your good understanding of PyTorch for model development, training, and optimization.
** Data Analysis and Algorithm Development: Apply your understanding of Data Structure and Algorithms (DSA) to create efficient solutions and analyze complex datasets.
** Collaborate with Cross-Functional Teams: Work closely with software engineers, data scientists, and product managers to ensure alignment with project goals.
** Conduct Research: Stay up to date with the latest advancements in AI, ML, NLP, and Computer Vision to bring innovation to our products.
** Maintain and Optimize Models: Monitor model performance, conduct regular updates, and ensure models adhere to industry best practices.
