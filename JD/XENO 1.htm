Job ProfileJob Profile:
** Partner with our clients to deliver predictable outcomes and experiences for their clients and predictable growth for their business by:
** Client Support: Assist Account Managers in providing excellent customer support to clients by addressing inquiries, resolving issues, and managing feedback promptly and professionally.
** Relationship Building: Work alongside the Account Managers to build strong, trust-based relationships with clients, understanding their objectives and identifying opportunities for upselling and cross-selling.
** Project Management: Collaborate with internal teams to ensure the timely and successful delivery of projects, products, and services according to client needs and objectives.
** Data Management: Maintain accurate and up-to-date client records, ensuring all interactions, communications, and agreements are properly documented in our CRM system.
** Market Research: Conduct research on industry trends, competitors, and best practices to support the development of client proposals and business strategies.
** Reporting and Analytics: Assist in preparing regular reports and performance metrics for clients, showcasing the value and impact of our solutions.
** Sales Support: Aid the sales team in lead generation, qualifying prospects, and assisting with the preparation of sales presentations.
** Training and Development: Engage in continuous learning and training opportunities to improve your account management skills and knowledge of our products/services.

What We’re Looking For:
** Education: Currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Marketing, or a related field. (Alternatively, specify if the position is open to recent graduates.)
** Excellent Communication Skills: Strong verbal and written communication skills to interact with clients and internal teams effectively.
** Customer Focus: A passion for delivering exceptional customer service and a genuine interest in understanding client needs.
** Problem-Solving Skills: Ability to think critically, identify potential issues, and propose creative solutions.
** Collaborative Nature: A team player who can work cohesively in a fast-paced environment and contribute to the success of the account management team.
** Tech-Savviness: Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite and familiarity with CRM software (e.g., Salesforce) is a plus.
** Adaptability: Ability to adapt quickly to changes and handle multiple tasks simultaneously.
