Job Profile

Job Description:
Job Title: Software Engineer
Who You Are:
** You want to be part of a team of the most talented, forward-thinking engineers in the industry
** You care about product ownership and solving problems for our customers
** You’re passionate about being in a product-focused environment where everyone cares deeply about customer impact
** You love the challenges that come with early stage engineering efforts and loves building stuff from scratch
** Have experience in professional React development around the user experience, an eye for design and attention to detail, and experience with responsive web design
** Have experience in professional Node.js development, building and consuming horizontally scalable APIs

Responsibilities:
** Have the ability to work with a design team and translate UI/UX design wireframes to scalable code
** Experience in developing pixel perfect frontend software, with deep experience in React.js
** Fluent with modern JavaScript and its build tools/architecture.
** Solid Understanding of HTTP protocol and how server-side code is processed on the client.
** Ensure optimal performance of the central database and responsiveness to front-end requests.
** Experience in optimizing CRUD of large amounts of data.Experience designing systems that deal with large data sets and a huge volume of transactions.
** Write reusable, scalable and testable code.
** Ability to work both autonomously and as part of a team, including the ability to collaborate closely with engineers in other locations.
** Take a collaborative approach to work and can incorporate feedback from product, design, and your engineering peers.
** Experience in CI/CD and server deployments in EC2 or any other cloud providers
** Experience in test-driven development and automated testing.
