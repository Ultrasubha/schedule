Job ProfileResponsibilities:
** Assist in the deployment and configuration of infrastructure using infrastructure-as-code (IaC) tools like Terraform or CloudFormation.
** Collaborate with the development team to streamline the software delivery process, including continuous integration and continuous deployment (CI/CD).
** Help manage and monitor cloud-based environments (AWS) to ensure availability, performance, and scalability.
** Assist in automating routine tasks and processes using scripting languages like Bash, Python, or PowerShell.
** Contribute to the development and improvement of monitoring and alerting systems to ensure system health and availability.
** Collaborate with team members to troubleshoot and resolve infrastructure and deployment-related issues.
** Learn and apply best practices for security, compliance, and data protection in the DevOps environment.
** Stay up to date with industry trends, tools, and technologies related to DevOps and cloud computing.

Requirements:
** Familiarity with basic concepts of DevOps, cloud computing, and infrastructure-as-code.
** Knowledge of at least one cloud platform (AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud) is preferred.
** Programming/scripting skills in languages such as Python, Java, Kotlin, Bash, Python, or PowerShell.
** Strong problem-solving and analytical skills.
** Ability to work effectively both independently and in a team environment.
** Good communication and interpersonal skills.
** Eagerness to learn and take on new challenges.
