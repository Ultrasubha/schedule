About Company

Its  Global Comany with Offices all around main many Countries . Securonix provides the Next Generation Security and Information Event Management (SIEM) solution. As a  recognized leader in the SIEM industry, Securonix helps some of largest organizations globally to detect  sophisticated cyberattacks and rapidly respond to these attacks within minutes. With the Securonix SNYPR  platform, organizations can collect billions of events each day and analyze them in near real time to detect  advanced persistent threats (APTs), insider threats, privilege account misuses and online fraud. Securonix  pioneered the User and Entity Behavior Analytics (UEBA) market and holds patents in the use of behavioral  algorithms to detect malicious activities. The Securonix SNYPR platform is built on big data Hadoop  technologies and is infinitely scalable. Our platform is used by some of the largest organizations in the  financial, healthcare, pharmaceutical, manufacturing, and federal sector.

Job Profile

Job Title: Software Engineer - Intern
Job Level: Individual Contributor
Primary Skills: Data Structures, Algorithms, OOPs, Logical Thinking Ability
Secondary Skills: Coding/Scripting Knowledge in Java/Python, Agile Methodology

POSITION RESPONSIBILITIES:
** Work with developers and management to design and implement delivery pipelines.
** Participate in evaluation and evangelize forward-looking processes, tools and technologies that help deliver high-quality secure software faster and more efficiently.
** Assist in Designing and developing supporting tools for NextGen Cyber Security Products.
** Assist in producing high quality software following good architecture and design principles that the team will find easy to work with in the future.
** Contribute in Design, develop, maintain automation frameworks to support the Cyber Security product which is processing terabytes of data.
** Assist in Researching and implementing code design, adoption of new technologies and skills.
** Assist in the documentation of software designs, technical specifications, and user guides.

REQUIRED SKILLS:
** You should have excellent problem-solving skills, Knowledge in Data Structure and programming in Java.
** Good understanding of OOP concepts
** Understanding of Python or any scripting language
** Awareness of automation/testing tools like Selenium, Cypress, Karate, TestNG, Cucumber, etc
** Knowledge of the Hadoop eco system would be an added advantage.
