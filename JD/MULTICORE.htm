About Company

We are a worldwide team of scientists and entrepreneurs focused on developing technologies for video codecs, media analytics, and autonomous vehicles. By leveraging our combined skills in high performance & heterogeneous computing, machine learning & neural networks, computer vision, and compiler technologies, we are uniquely positioned to advance the state-of-the-art in our focus areas. We are member of the OpenCL Consortium as well as member of HSA (Heterogeneous System Architecture) foundation. MulticoreWare, Inc was founded in 2009 and has around 300 employees across different locations in USA, China and India.
