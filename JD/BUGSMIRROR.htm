Bugsmirror is a growing organization acknowledged by both Google and Samsung which works in the field of enhancing the security of the Android Operating System and iOS. Bugsmirror has been contributing to Android security and privacy for almost 4 years now and Google acknowledged our role as crucial in their Blog post in February 2022 and 2023.
