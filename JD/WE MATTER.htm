Job ProfileBack End Developer Intern (With a PPO Option)
Job Description:
** Develop and maintain server-side logic using Node.js, JavaScript, and Python.
** Collaborate with front-end developers, designers, and product managers to create scalable and responsive web applications.
** Design and implement APIs and web services to facilitate seamless integration with other systems.
** Optimize applications for maximum speed, scalability, and reliability.
** Identify and troubleshoot performance bottlenecks and implement effective solutions.
** Ensure the security and data protection of the backend systems.
** Write efficient and reusable code, following industry best practices and coding standards.
** Conduct code reviews and provide constructive feedback to improve code quality.
** Stay up-to-date with the latest industry trends and technologies to drive continuous improvement.
** Participate in the entire software development lifecycle, from requirements gathering to deployment and maintenance.

Requirements:
** Final year student of Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field.
** Proficiency in Node.js, JavaScript, and PHP.
** Exposure to backend frameworks such as Express.js or Flask.
** Familiarity with database systems such as MongoDB and MySQL.
** Understanding of version control systems (e.g., Git) and collaborative development workflows.
** Exposure to cloud platforms such as AWS or GCP is preferred but not mandatory.
** Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.
** Strong communication and collaboration abilities.
** Ability to work in a fast-paced and agile environment.
** Attention to detail and commitment to writing clean, maintainable code.
** Knowledge of software testing methodologies and tools is a plus.

Only those candidates can apply who:
** Have relevant skills and interests.
** Have a laptop not older than 4 years.

Front End Developer Intern (With a PPO option)
Job Description:
** Build new user-facing features using React.js and other front-end technologies.
** Develop reusable components and libraries for future use.
** Collaborate with the development team to design and implement RESTful APIs.
** Translate designs and wireframes into high-quality code.
** Create responsive HTML designs based on wireframes built on PSD, Figma, and ETC. to work across mobile and web browsers.
** Develop and implement front-end architectures and design patterns.
** Stay up-to-date with emerging trends and technologies in front-end development.
** Write unit tests and integration tests to ensure code quality.
** Ensure code follows best practices and coding standards.
** Work collaboratively with UX designers and product owners to ensure a seamless user experience.

Skill(s) required:
** Proficiency in React.js and its core principles.
** Exposure to state management tools such as Redux and Saga.
** Proficient in HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript/ Typescript
** Exposure to responsive design and cross-browser compatibility.
** Knowledge of RESTful APIs and how to integrate them into front-end applications.
** Exposure to Material-UI or similar component libraries.
** Exposure to Git and similar version control like SVN.
** Familiar in writing unit test cases
** Ability to write clean, reusable, and maintainable code.
** Excellent problem-solving skills and ability to work independently or as part of a team.
** Strong communication skills and ability to collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams.
** Exposure to extended frameworks like Next.js, Vue.js, and Nuxt.js.

Only those candidates can apply who:
** Have relevant skills and interests.
** Have a laptop not older than 4 years.
