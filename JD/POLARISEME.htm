Salary Details :
** Starting monthly stipend of INR 50K, which will be increased to INR 55K as soon as candidate achieves Appian Associate Developer Credentials (expectation is within the first month after joining), and further increased to INR 65K as soon as candidate achieves Appian Senior Developer Credentials (expectations is within first three to six months after joining). This will bring the base salary to approx. INR 8 Lac per annum. that the candidate can achieve within three to six months of joining based on achieving independently administered Appian credentials exams.  
** Productivity bonus – Once candidate is trained and staffed on a Times & Materials billable project, and upon Manager’s discretion, the candidate would be eligible to additional INR, 1,000 per full day of billable work as reported in relevant Time Reporting systems, which can amount up to an additional INR 21,000 per month of productivity bonus. The productivity bonus will be payable in the subsequent month to the reporting period month provided candidate’s Employment status continues to be active with PolariseMe.
** End of Year Performance Bonus - Additionally, based on candidate’s performance, overall company performance, and Manager’s discretion, candidate will be eligible for a performance bonus of up to INR 1.25L at end of company fiscal year.
