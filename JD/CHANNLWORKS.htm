About Company

Channlworks is a next generation Alliance Management Platform that enables B2B SaaS companies and their channel ecosystem to collaborate and jointly go-to-market(GTM) to co-sell, co-deliver and co-market the best in class solutions to their customers. Channlworks's mission is to democratise alliance management where both the vendors and their partners find more value when they collaborate across various facets of the relationship. Channlworks is the brainchild of industry veterans with multiple decades of channel and alliance management experience between them across global IT giants like AWS, Facebook, IBM etc. We are growing fast, have a nimble (but super efficient) team, flexible working policies and open work environment which empowers individuals and brings the best out of them.

Job Profile

The Role:
We believe that ultimately, the role of the Software Engineer is to build high-quality, fully performing software. At Channlworks, the responsibilities of a software engineer would include gathering user requirements, defining functionality and writing code in various languages; e.g. JavaScript/TypeScript. We are looking for passionate IT professionals as interns who can develop applications and APIs for web/mobile architectures.

Preferred Skills:
** Experience with Node JS API framework using Express JS.
** Experience in developing web pages using REACT JS and a strong knowledge of CSS.
** Experience in relational databases preferably PostgreSQL.
** Should have experience in any one of the version control frameworks(GitLab or GitHub ).
** Familiarity with the software development life cycles (SDLC) from preliminary system analysis to tests and deployment.
