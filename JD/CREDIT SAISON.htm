About Company

A Neo-Lending Conglomerate here to enable India's credit growth story, Credit Saison India, emboldened by our Global MNC parent, is here to accelerate India's credit growth story: by building a Technology-Led Neo-Lending Conglomerate. Across our verticals, we offer bespoke solutions to meet the various credit needs of Individuals, SMEs, FinTech's, and NBFCs. Credit Saison India (“CS India”), having been founded in 2018, operates under the registered name Kisetsu Saison Finance India Private Limited as an MNC subsidiary of its parent company – Credit SaisonCo. Ltd in Japan. Across our various business verticals like Wholesale Financing, Co-origination Financing, Consumer Financing, and SME Financing we look towards reaching resilient Assets Under Management (“AUM”) of US1bn in record time.

Job ProfileJob Profile:
** financial analysis
** Sales & Marketing of Financial Products/Services and Industry Research

Key Responsibilities:
** Participate in an intensive training program covering various aspects of small business loans, sales techniques, financial analysis, and industry regulations.
** Learn to effectively communicate the features and benefits of our small business loan products to potential clients.
** Collaborate with senior sales professionals to identify and target prospective small business clients within assigned territories.
** Conduct thorough financial assessments of potential clients to determine their eligibility for loans and recommend appropriate loan structures.
** Develop and maintain strong relationships with clients, providing exceptional customer service throughout the sales process and beyond.
** Assist in the preparation and submission of loan applications, ensuring accuracy and completeness of documentation.
** Stay updated with industry trends, market developments, and competitive landscape to effectively position our loan products.
** Achieve individual and team sales targets, contributing to the company's growth and success.
** Provide regular reports and updates on sales activities, pipeline progress, and market feedback to the sales management team.

Skills Set Required:
** Detail-oriented and proficient in financial analysis.
** Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
** Proficiency in Microsoft Office suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint).
