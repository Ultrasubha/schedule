About Company

KPIT Technologies Limited (formerly KPIT Cummins Infosystems Ltd) is an Indian multinational corporation headquartered in Pune, Maharashtra, India.  Popularly known as KPIT, it provides software to the automotive companies. KPIT has development centers in Europe, USA, Japan, China, Thailand, and India. Mobility is going through paradigm shifts, led by adoption of new technologies and rapid increase of embedded software in vehicles. Software craftsmanship can enable mobility (and the world) leapfrog towards a clean, safe and smarter future. KPIT specializes in embedded software, AI & digital solutions for the automotive and mobility industry enabling our customers accelerate implementation of next generation mobility technologies and our passionate employees to work with the coolest tech and the top brands in the industry.With technology centers in Europe, USA, Japan, China & India – KPIT is present where the mobility ecosystem is transforming.
