About Company

i2V stands for Intelligent Integrated Video. i2V is a technology R&D company focusing on design, development and production of Intelligent Surveillance solutions. i2V specializes in Proactive Video Surveillance products with an aim of providing high quality software to clients worldwide.

Websitehttps://www.i2vsys.com
Job ProfileRoles And Responsibilities:
** You will be responsible for development and optimization of various computer vision algorithms/pipelines for video surveillance
** This includes, but is not limited to: Development of object detection, classification, tracking, and counting algorithms
** Porting of already existing algorithms/pipelines to GPU
** Optimization of already existing cpu/gpu algorithms/pipelines

Required Skills:
** High proficiency in C, C++
** Strong understanding of image processing, computer vision algorithms and machine learning
** High competency with image processing, computer vision and machine learning libraries such as OpenCV, Keras, OpenVino, Deepstream, Pytorch, Tensorflow
** Experience with rapid prototyping with e.g. Python, OpenCV
** Previous work in video surveillance is a big plus
** Should work independently as well as with team

There will be a total three rounds of interview:
1st round: Online assessment test (computer Vision Assessment test)
** In the assessment, you will find detailed instructions about how to complete the assessment.
** Please make sure to read all the instructions carefully as they will help you to complete the assessment more successfully.
** In this assessment, we want to evaluate the skills and programming knowledge of the candidates. For this assessment, every candidate must have a laptop and webcam.
** The test includes general aptitude MCQ, & some technology-related questions.
** The duration of the test is about 1hr24mins. Candidates can try to give the sample test first to understand the assessment.

2nd round: Technical round
** In the Technical round, we want to evaluate your technical skills.
** In this round, you will be asked your profile-related questions and other important technical knowledge that will be related to the position that we are hiring for. 
** we will give you time in the next 1 working day to complete a model and submit it and explain the technologies used in it in the next virtual interview.

3rd round: HR round
** This is the final part of the procedure.
** In this round, you will be meeting with the company's CEO.
** In this round candidates will be judged by their confidence and abilities. 
** All rounds of the interviews are elimination rounds.
