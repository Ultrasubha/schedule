About Company

Skillish is a one-stop for the one who is seeking internships and programs. We aim at providing our students with the best training to make them skilled and experts and then lend them amazing job opportunities to upskill their talent. Our organization and the MNC will lend the student the two live projects, they will work under guidance to gain experience. To make our students have high earnings and high postings in the job, we provide them with 3 certificates after the completion of their course to enhance or upgrade their CV. We give a letter of recommendation to make your work easier while at your job or when you go abroad to study.We, at Skillsh, provide the students with 3-4 live sessions weekly that help students to clarify their doubts and learn more efficiently. Usually, the online session runs for 1 to 2 hours in the evening. The live sessions are being recorded which enables the students who missed the class to have a look at the previously uploaded videos that will be posted after the live session. If you are unable to understand, we can also arrange a live doubt session to make you clear about everything. Our experts are the ones that have 8 – 10 years of experience in their respective fields as we are tied up with the popular and excellent MNCs.In Skillish we usually provide 3-month training in which the initial 2 months are being used for training and in the 3rd month we provide them with an internship. Usually, we deal in different domains to satisfy every want of the student that desires to be a professional and skilled person having expertise and perfection in their work. Our domains are Civil Engineering, Computer Science, Medical courses, management, etc.
