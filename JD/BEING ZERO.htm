About Company

Being Zero is a growing ed-tech startup which specializes in skilling candidates for product based companies through its hands-on skilling sessions on Full Stack Development and Data Structures and Algorithms.  Company operates in telangana and has a great success rate with many candidates skilled by Being Zero working with top MNCs like - Microsoft, Google, Amazon, Salesforce, Oracle, Adobe, Walmart, to name a few.  Company is founded by ex-Microsoft, ex-Google employees having more than 10 years of experience.

Websitehttps://beingzero.in/
Job ProfileJob Description:
** Candidates are expected to contribute to impactful work Being Zero performs in skilling students on Data Structures Algorithms and Full Stack Development.

Key Responsibilities:
** Contributing to Design, Development and Deployment of Online Coding Platform, Analytics Platform and Other tools being built to manage, monitor and track the progress of cohorts. (mentorpick.com, https://bzcptracker.mentorpick.com)
** Teaching and Managing batch of students for Data Structures and Algorithms.
** Tracking and Monitoring progress of candidates.
**  Answering doubts on data structures and algorithmic problems raised by candidates.
** Mentor students and conduct regular follow-up sessions with students to discuss their progress and plan to take them to the next level.
** Author coding contests to assess skills of candidates.
** Host sessions to discuss approaches and solutions to coding contest problems.
** Participate and promote participation in coding contests hosted by third party sites like LeetCode, CodeChef and Codeforces.
** Contribute to increase the effective outcome for each candidate enrolled in the batches.

Must have Skill:
** Strong DSA, MERN Stack, Strong interest in teaching.
** Good to have skills: OOPS, Prior experience in conducting sessions/webinars/public speaking/YouTube videos creation/blog writing will be an added advantage

Required Qualifications Are:
** Graduates with great Academic track record in MTech, MCA, BTech in CSE/IT, graduating in 2024.
** Exceptional communication and interpersonal skills.
** Strong track record in competitive programming on leading coding platforms (CodeChef/Codeforces/InterviewBit/LeetCode/HackerRank/HackerEarth etc).
** Sound knowledge of application of object oriented programming concepts.
** Prior experience in conducting sessions/webinars/public speaking/Youtube videos creation/blog.
** Hands-on experience with any one full web stack (MERN/MEAN or similar)
